Become a Friend of Milarepa Center today!


Sign up today as a New Friend to become eligible to win a free two-night retreat in a private cabin!*

Milarepa Center is a special place dedicated to inspiring the highest potential in all beings through the cultivation of wisdom, compassion and the good heart. Situated on 276 acres of serene, Vermont beauty, we provide a variety of programs for all levels. Milarepa Center simply wouldn’t exist without the kind and generous support of so many of you. We cannot thank you enough.

 

 

But we still need help!

Between February 16th and March 15th we are on a mission to double our Friend base.
Will you become one of our 35 New Friends?

When you Become a Friend you directly ensure that Milarepa Center has a steady stream of financial support necessary for the Center to function and thrive month by month.

All of our dear Friends automatically receive enhanced benefits with their Friendship level options, including a FREE Mandala Magazine subscription, library borrowing privileges, and across-the-board discounts on programs, retreat cabins, and the Gift Shop. We encourage you to explore these wonderful opportunities for your Dharma practice, and visit us often! View the Friendship Benefits Chart

And now, Become a Friend on-or-before the March 15th deadline, and you’re instantly entered into the…

BECOME A FRIEND FREE RETREAT DRAWING!

1st Prize
  • FREE Two Night Personal Retreat Cabin (value $160)
  • Just added! FREE Day Retreat with Master Teacher (value $40)
2nd Prize
  • Just added! FREE One Night Retreat Cabin (value $80)
  • FREE Day Retreat with Master Teacher (value $40)

New Friend Bonus: Just by signing up on-or-before the deadline, you’ll receive a FREE powerful Namgyalma mantra sticker for your home or car.

So please Become a Friend today! You’ll have the deep satisfaction of knowing you are part of Milarepa’s core vision of benefiting all beings. Thank you.
