Practice Days

General

Group practice leads to a greater sense of overall harmony. These practices lend benefit in clearing obstacles to different activities (Tara Puja), for healing (Medicine Buddha Puja), and for overall center health and harmony (Tsog, etc.). We invite you to join us for the various rituals or pujas we have scheduled. These practice days are open to all levels of practitioners. It's very easy to participate; you don't need to know the practices in advance as we offer explanation and have practice booklets available. It's nice to bring abundant offerings that can be set up on the altar and shared with others such as fruit, chips, pretzels, and any other snacks you enjoy. Flowers are always welcome.

Tara Puja
8th day of Tibetan calendar

Four Mandala Offerings to Cittamani Tara is a great way to clear out obstacles to work, relationships, and general disharmony in your life.

Milarepa Tsog
25th day of Tibetan calendar

A tsog is an offering ceremony that represents assembling all of the Buddhas and Bodhisattvas in a visualization of a sacred dance. Those practicing highest yoga tantra normally have a commitment to do this practice twice each month. Lama Zopa Rinpoche, Milarepa Center's Spiritual Director, has encouraged us to do the Milarepa Tsog, a beautiful practice. All are welcome!

Lama Chopa Tsog
10th day of Tibetan calendar

The Lama Chopa practice is a profound Guru Yoga practice that connects us more deeply to our teachers and the inspiration of the buddhas.

Medicine Buddha Puja
15th day of Tibetan calendar

A tremendous healing practice, Medicine Buddha is seen as the King of Doctors. This practice can be enormously helpful for your own illness or perfect to participate with someone else in mind.

Puja Fund

If you would like a puja done for a specific purpose or person, click the "Donate Now" button to make an offering to the "Puja Fund." Then send us an email with the name of the person or purpose and we will do a specific practice for you. The amount of the donation is entirely up to you.
