Transportation

Milarepa Center is located in northeastern Vermont on the New Hampshire border, one hour north of White River Junction off of Interstate 91. We are located approximately one hour south of the Canadian border and three hours northwest of Boston.

Our address is:
1344 US Route 5 South
Barnet, Vermont 05821

Below are a variety of transportation options to bring you to Milarepa Center. However, if you have pre-registered for a program, please check with us about anyone registered from your area for carpooling purposes.

  • Driving

    From the south, on Interstate 91, take Exit 18. Turn right at the exit and take the first left onto Church Street until it ends at a stop sign (the Barnet Village Store will be on your right). Turn left onto US 5 (which isnâ€™t marked). Go half a mile, just after going under the two underpasses, turn hard left into the driveway marked by a Milarepa sign with prayer banners.

    From the north, on Interstate 91, take Exit 18. Turn left at the exit and follow the instructions above taking the first left onto Church Street.

    Please note: Our driveway is unpaved and quite steep. During the winter season we keep our driveway plowed at all times and sanded for guests arriving for retreat. Please exercise the same caution used to drive in winter conditions.

  • Car Rentals

    Enterprise Rental Car has nearby branches in St. Johnsbury, Vermont and Burlington, Vermont. For more information, visit their website, or call (800) 261-7331.

  • Air Travel

    Here are the nearest major airports, and our suggestions for ground travel to Milarepa:

    Logan International Airport - Boston, MA
    Distance from Milarepa: about 3 hours by car
    Recommended ground transportation:

    1. Dartmouth Coach from Logan Airport to the Hanover Inn in Hanover, New Hampshire
    Cost: about $38 one way

    Or Take Concord Coach from Logan Airport to Littlenton, NH
    Cost: about $37 one way

    Then take:

    2. Rural Community Transport (RCT) from the Hanover Inn to the Barnet Park and Ride
    Cost: about $3.00 one way, but only operates Monday through Friday.

    If arriving on weekend, take the Dartmouth Coach from Logan Airport to Hanover or Lebanon, New Hampshire

    Then take:
    3. Apex Car Service to Milarepa

    Base rate of $138 for 1 or 2 people. Third person is an additional $30. In addition, there will also be a 20% driver gratuity added to the base rate and a $6 per car fuel fee.

    Burlington International Airport - Burlington, Vermont
    Distance from Milarepa: about 2 hours by car
    Recommended ground transportation:

    1. Vermont Tour and Charter from Burlington Airport to Milarepa
      Cost: $130 one way; $20 for each additional passenger

    Manchester Boston Regional Airport - Manchester, New Hampshire
    Distance from Milarepa: about 2 hours 15 minutes by car
    Recommended ground transportation:

    1. Greyhound Bus (Vermont Transit) from Manchester to White River Junction Cost: $23.75 to $26.50 one way. Best is to call the local number (802) 295-3011 for fares and schedules as the Greyhound website does not have the information.

    Then take:

    1. Apex Car Service from White River Junction to Milarepa
      Base rate of $138 for 1 or 2 people. Third person is an additional $30. In addition, there will also be a 20% driver gratuity added to the base rate and a $6 per car fuel fee.

    Lebanon Regional Airport - Lebanon, New Hampshire
    Distance from Milarepa - about 1 hour by car
    Recommended ground transportation:

    1. Apex Car Service from Lebanon Airport to Milarepa
      Base rate of $138 for 1 or 2 people. Third person is an additional $30. In addition, there will also be a 20% driver gratuity added to the base rate and a $6 per car fuel fee.

  • Ground Transportation

    Greyhound Bus (Vermont Transit) from Manchester to White River Junction Cost: $23.75 to $26.50 one way. Best is to call the local number (802) 295-3011 for fares and schedules as the Greyhound website does not always have all of the information.

    Arrive: Magog, Quebec

  • Regional Transportation Services

    Once you are in the area, these services can transport you directly to Milarepa.

    Apex Car Service (website)

    Apex offers rides to Milarepa Center with pickup from White River Junction, Vermont and Hanover or Lebanon, New Hampshire. Base rate of $138 for 1 or 2 people. Third person is an additional $30. In addition, there will also be a 20% driver gratuity added to the base rate and a $6 per car fuel fee. Apex accepts credit cards.

    To make pickup arrangements with Apex, call their reservations line at (603) 252-8294. Arrangements should be made at least 48 hours in advance. The minimum advance time to schedule a pickup is 24 hours.

    Rural Community Transportation (website)

    Rural Community Transportation (RCT) offers shuttle service between Milarepa and most nearby towns. Common towns for pickup include: White River Junction, Montpelier, and Burlington, Vermont and Hanover and Lebanon, New Hampshire; and Magog, Quebec.

    Cost for one-way travel to Milarepa varies by pickup location and number of passengers: approximately $35 each for two people from White River Junction or Hanover (or $70 for one person); $55 each for two people from Burlington (or $110 for one person), etc. Please inquire about the cost when you make your reservation.

    RCT also operates a regular shuttle service from White River Junction and St. Johnsbury (the closest town to Milarepa) to the Barnet Park and Ride (the closest stop to Milarepa) so check their website for scheduled times and costs.

    Vermont Tour and Charter - Burlington pickups (website)

    Vermont Tour and Charter offers pickup from Burlington International Airport and delivery to Milarepa. One-way fee is $130.

    Reserve a shuttle through their website, or call (802) 734-1293. Give at least 48 hours advance notice.

If you have additional questions about arranging transportation to or from Milarepa Center, please call (802) 633-4136 or email us at milarepa@milarepacenter.org.
