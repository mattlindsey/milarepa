Planned & Estate Giving

Milarepa Center relies on the kindness and generosity of others to secure our long-term strength and support our vision. Consider joining the community of people who want to ensure that the retreat and program activities of Milarepa Center flourish for generations to come.
It’s very easy to make a lasting gift to Milarepa Center. Just include Milarepa Center as a beneficiary

  • in your will or living trust
  • in your retirement plan or bank account
  • in your life insurance policy.

In addition, we would like to publish your name as it encourages others to provide similar support, or you may remain anonymous. If you have named or would like to include Milarepa Center in your estate planning, please contact Director, Amy Miller at (802) 633-4136 or at milarepa@milarepacenter.org.

We have an Estate Planning Form with sample bequest language and information which you can download as a PDF, or you may contact your attorney. We can also provide you with a copy of our IRS 501(c)(3) letter.


Milarepa Center, Inc., is qualified as a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Contributions are tax-deductible, as allowed by the law. No taxable goods or services are provided in consideration of gifts, unless stated otherwise.
