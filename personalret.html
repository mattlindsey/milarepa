Personal Retreats

Milarepa Center is currently under going some fundamental upgrades to enhance your retreat experience. What this means is, we are not hosting personal retreaters or group retreats at the moment. If you are not on our mailing list, sign up here to get our updates on our project.

Milarepa Center has private cabins and rooms for those needing a get-away or space for a private retreat. Vegetarian meals and bedding are provided with your cabin/room rental. Breakfast is served in the main dining hall at 8am and lunch and dinner are delivered to your cabin. We can accommodate vegan and gluten-free diets. Please let us know if you have other health related dietary restrictions and we can work with you.

Chenrezig

This cabin is available year-round for any type of retreat. It is close the main house but tucked away in the forest. This is our largest cabin with a bed and desk. The bathroom and shower are located in the main house which is just a short walk across the yard. There are no cooking facilities so food is delivered.

Yamantaka

Available year-round for any type of retreat. A favorite among retreaters as it is the cabin Lama Zopa Rinpoche stayed in during a retreat. It is a 2 minute walk to the main house, located in a private area overlooking a beautiful meadow. This cabin is quite small but very cozy, with a bed and small shelves for storage. There is an outhouse next to the cabin. There are bathrooms and showers located in the main house. There are no cooking facilities so food is delivered.

 

Manjushri

This rustic summer cabin is available for intensive retreat. It is a 15 minute walk from the main house with no plumbing or electricity. Water and food is delivered to retreaters.

This cabin was built by Venerable Roger Munro and Venerable Paula Chichester in the early 90's for their four-year Great Retreat. Due to their intense practice for four years, the staff and visitors to Milarepa Center who know their story, find this area very inspiring.
